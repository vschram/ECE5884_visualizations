The product-to-sum identities introduce a factor of 1/2; multiplying by 2 restores the I or Q amplitude after ideal low-pass filtering. Exact recovery here assumes a phase- and frequency-synchronised local oscillator, ideal low-pass filtering, no channel or noise distortion, and constant I and Q over the displayed symbol interval.
