MRT phasor alignment

Adjust the channel magnitudes and deliberately add transmit phase errors. Maximum-ratio transmission makes all received contributions point in the same direction.

Channel and beamforming controls

The weights remain unit norm. Only the phase error changes, so the SNR loss is caused by imperfect coherent addition rather than extra transmit power.
