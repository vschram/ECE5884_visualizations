Simultaneous equal-power spatial repetition
heff = (h₁ + h₂)/√2

The √2 normalisation keeps the total transmitted symbol energy equal to Ex. The random button draws independent CN(0,1) channels and quantises their displayed magnitude and phase to the slider resolution.
