Common misconception: "More power always means more capacity." At high SNR, doubling transmit power adds only ~1 bit/s/Hz (log₂ 2 = 1). Bandwidth is the more efficient lever — but spectrum is scarce and licensed. This fundamental trade-off motivates the entire design space of wireless systems.
